Who we are...

Passion, cinema and community: The legacy of the Ghostbusters lives on.
We are a non-profit organization dedicated to paying tribute to the legendary Ghostbusters saga. We faithfully recreate the costumes and gadgets that appear in the films, and we bring that cinematic experience to life through events, activities, and audience engagements. Our mission is to keep the spirit of the original '80s films alive: to awaken nostalgia in those who grew up with them and to spread the passion for this universe to new generations. We are officially licensed by GHOSTCORPS, which certifies our commitment to authenticity and respect for the franchise.

    What we do...

    We bring the Ghostbusters universe to your event!
    We participate in all types of events where needed: trade shows, presentations, private parties, children's events, themed events, and much more. We have a fully interactive booth, packed with unique items from the Ghostbusters universe: detailed replicas, comics, classic toys, audiovisual material, and gadgets that will delight both longtime fans and little ones. Whether you're trying to liven up a celebration or add a special touch to a large event, our team will make the experience unforgettable!

    Ghostbusters with heart!
    In addition to participating in events and recreational activities, our association also believes in the power of solidarity.
